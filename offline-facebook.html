Facebook without Internet made possible






Install the application without internet

Indian markets are flooded with shops which provide usb sticks full of movies, music, and software for people to purchase. Some of them even have subscriptions models so that people without internet can stay upto date with new content. Users can go to these physical hotspot shops and download latest android or iPhone apps as they desire. Tie-ups with such hotspots or self-creation of them can enable incentivizing to go back for updates.

Interacting with application without internet
