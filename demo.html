P2Feed

We have built a mock-up of the Facebook Android application that enables peer-to-peer connections.
This application allows a user to download posts from their peers in addition to Facebook servers.

  • Blue Posts

  • Indicates a post was downloaded from Facebook

  • Green Posts

  • Indicates a post was obtained from a peer, that was originally intended to be in the users' feed

  • Select Data Usage Mode

  • Allows the selection of a mode to determine how much data the phone uses and the kind of posts shown in the feed.

  • Yellow Posts

  • Indicates a post was obtained from a peer and is either a public post or a post by a mutual friend

Data Usage Mode Selector

Original

 
Steps
  1. A downloads news feed from Facebook
  2. B downloads news feed from Facebook
Data Types Received

Reduced

 
Steps
  1. A downloads news feed from Facebook
  2. B downloads news feed post IDs from Facebook
  3. B sends news feed post IDs to A
  4. A sends overlapping news feed posts to B
  5. B downloads non-overlapping news feed posts from Facebook
Data Types Received

Minimal

 
Steps
  1. A downloads news feed from Facebook
  2. B downloads news feed post IDs from Facebook
  3. B sends news feed post IDs and list of friends to A
  4. A sends overlapping news feed posts as well as public and mutual friend posts to B
Data Types Received

Offline

 
Steps
  1. A downloads news feed from Facebook
  2. B sends list of friends to A
  3. A sends public and mutual friend posts to B
Data Types Received
